# Airlines Pilot Guide

Complete guide to the Los Santos Airlines pilot career.

## Overview

As a pilot for Los Santos Airlines, you'll transport passengers and cargo between airports across San Andreas. This job offers variety with passenger flights, cargo hauls, private charters, and emergency dispatch missions.

## Getting Started

### Requirements
- Valid pilot's license (obtained through Flight School)
- Clean criminal record preferred
- Basic flight knowledge

### How to Join
1. Visit Los Santos International Airport (LSIA)
2. Find the Airlines office near the terminals
3. Speak to the hiring NPC
4. Complete flight school if needed

## Job Grades

| Grade | Title | Duties |
|-------|-------|--------|
| 0 | Trainee | Flight school, observation |
| 1 | Pilot | All flight operations |
| 2 | Senior Pilot/Boss | Management, hiring |

## Flight School

Before you can fly passengers, complete three training lessons:

### Lesson 1: Basic Flight
- Takeoff procedures
- Level flight
- Basic navigation
- Landing approach

### Lesson 2: Advanced Maneuvers
- Weather handling
- Emergency procedures
- Instrument flying
- Night operations

### Lesson 3: Commercial Operations
- Passenger protocols
- Cargo procedures
- ATC communication
- Route planning

**Cost:** Training fees apply
**Reward:** Pilot's license upon completion

## Airports

### Main Hub
- **Los Santos International (LSIA)** - Full services, all aircraft

### Regional Airports
- **Sandy Shores Airfield** - Small/medium aircraft
- **Grapeseed Airstrip** - Small aircraft only
- **Fort Zancudo** - Military, restricted access

### Custom Airports
- **Roxwood International** - Full international
- **Paleto Regional** - Regional flights

## Flight Types

### Passenger Flights
- Transport NPC passengers between airports
- Payment based on distance and passenger count
- Reputation bonus for smooth flights

### Cargo Transport
- Haul freight between locations
- Weight-based payments
- Special cargo bonuses

### Private Charters
- VIP player transport
- Custom pickup/dropoff locations
- Premium pricing

### Emergency Dispatch
- Urgent transport requests
- Time-sensitive missions
- Bonus pay for quick completion

## Flight Procedures

### Pre-Flight
1. Clock in at the dispatch desk
2. Check available flights on the job board
3. Accept a flight assignment
4. Spawn your aircraft at the designated spot
5. Fuel up if needed (qs-fuel integration)
6. Request ATC clearance

### During Flight
1. Board passengers/load cargo
2. Follow your flight plan
3. Monitor weather conditions
4. Maintain communication
5. Handle any delays professionally

### Post-Flight
1. Land safely at destination
2. Deplane passengers/unload cargo
3. Complete flight log
4. Collect payment
5. Return or store aircraft

## Weather System

Weather affects flight operations:

| Condition | Impact |
|-----------|--------|
| Clear | Normal operations |
| Cloudy | Reduced visibility |
| Rain | Delayed departures |
| Storm | Flights grounded |
| Fog | Approach difficulties |

## Maintenance

Aircraft require regular maintenance:
- Service after X flights
- Repair damage promptly
- Pre-flight inspections
- Report issues to mechanics

## Boss Menu (Grade 2+)

Senior pilots can access:
- Employee management
- Company finances
- Flight statistics
- Hiring/firing

## Tips for Success

1. **Complete flight school** - Required for commercial flights
2. **Check weather** - Avoid flying in storms
3. **Build reputation** - Better assignments with experience
4. **Fuel management** - Don't run out mid-flight
5. **Communication** - Use radio for ATC/dispatch
6. **Maintenance** - Keep aircraft in good condition
