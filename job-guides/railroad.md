# Railroad & Trains Guide

Complete guide to railroad operations on DPSRP.

## Overview

DPSRP uses **Ehbw-Trains** for a realistic train system with engineer jobs, passenger and freight operations.

## Train Operations

### Types of Trains
| Type | Purpose | Requirements |
|------|---------|--------------|
| Freight | Cargo transport | Railroad job |
| Passenger | People transport | Railroad job |
| Metro | City transit | Automated |

## Getting the Railroad Job

### Requirements
- Apply at railroad depot
- Clean driving record preferred
- Complete training

### Job Location
- **Railroad Depot:** Near industrial area
- **Train Yards:** Multiple locations

## Engineer Duties

### Pre-Departure Checklist
1. Clock in at depot
2. Inspect locomotive
3. Check route assignment
4. Verify cargo/passengers
5. Get clearance to depart

### Operating the Train

#### Controls
| Key | Action |
|-----|--------|
| W | Throttle up |
| S | Throttle down/Brake |
| Space | Emergency brake |
| H | Horn |
| L | Lights |

### Speed Limits
| Area | Speed Limit |
|------|-------------|
| Yards | 15 mph |
| Urban | 35 mph |
| Rural | 55 mph |
| Express | 70+ mph |

## Freight Operations

### Cargo Types
- Containers
- Bulk materials
- Vehicles
- Hazardous materials (certified only)

### Freight Routes
| Route | Start | End | Distance |
|-------|-------|-----|----------|
| Port Run | LS Port | Sandy | Medium |
| Mountain | LS | Paleto | Long |
| Industrial | LS | Harmony | Short |

### Freight Pay
- Based on cargo type
- Distance multiplier
- On-time bonus
- Safe delivery bonus

## Passenger Operations

### Station Stops
- Los Santos Central
- Del Perro
- Sandy Shores
- Paleto Bay

### Passenger Duties
1. Arrive at station on time
2. Wait for passengers to board
3. Announce departures
4. Maintain schedule
5. Safe stops at each station

### Schedule Adherence
- Early departures penalized
- Late arrivals reduce pay
- Punctuality bonuses

## Safety Procedures

### Rail Crossings
- Sound horn approaching crossings
- Maintain speed limits
- Watch for vehicles on tracks

### Emergency Procedures
| Emergency | Action |
|-----------|--------|
| Track obstruction | Emergency brake, report |
| Mechanical failure | Stop, call dispatch |
| Derailment | Emergency stop, evacuate |

### Reporting
- All incidents reported to dispatch
- Document unusual events
- Safety first always

## Train Yard Operations

### Switching
- Move cars between tracks
- Assemble trains
- Requires precision

### Coupling/Uncoupling
1. Approach target car slowly
2. Use coupling controls
3. Verify secure connection
4. Test brakes

## Roleplay Tips

### Authentic Railroad RP
- Use proper radio protocol
- Announce horn signals
- Follow dispatcher instructions
- Communicate with station staff

### Horn Signals
| Signal | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| Long-Long-Short-Long | Approaching crossing |
| Short-Short | Acknowledgment |
| Long | Departing |
| Short | Stop |

## Tips for Success

1. **Learn the tracks** - Know the routes
2. **Watch speed** - Curves require slowing
3. **Use horn** - Safety at crossings
4. **Stay alert** - Long routes require focus
5. **Communicate** - Keep dispatch informed
6. **Be patient** - Trains are slow to stop
7. **Schedule aware** - Punctuality matters
