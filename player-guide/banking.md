# Banking Guide

![Maze Bank](../images/banking/mazebank.webp)

*The financial heart of Los Santos*

Managing your money in Los Santos.

## Maze Bank

The primary banking institution in Los Santos.

### Locations
- **Main Branch** - Downtown Los Santos
- **Branches** - Throughout the city
- **ATMs** - Widely available

## Basic Services

### Deposits
1. Visit a bank or ATM
2. Select "Deposit"
3. Enter amount
4. Confirm transaction

### Withdrawals
1. Visit a bank or ATM
2. Select "Withdraw"
3. Enter amount
4. Receive cash

### Transfers
Send money to other players:
1. Open banking menu
2. Select "Transfer"
3. Enter player ID or name
4. Enter amount
5. Confirm transfer

## Account Types

### Cash
- Physical money on your person
- Can be lost if robbed/killed
- Used for street transactions

### Bank
- Secure digital funds
- Cannot be stolen directly
- Used for major purchases

## Phone Banking

![Mobile Banking](../images/phone/using%20phone2.jpg)

*Bank on the go*

Access your account via the lb-phone banking app:
- Check balance
- Recent transactions
- Quick transfers
- Bill payments

## Business Banking

For job bosses and business owners:
- Society/company accounts
- Payroll management
- Expense tracking

## Tips

1. **Keep cash low** - Deposit regularly to stay safe
2. **Use bank for big purchases** - Cars, houses, etc.
3. **Check your balance** - Don't overdraft
4. **Save money** - Build up for future goals
