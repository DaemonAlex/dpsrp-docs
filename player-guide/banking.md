## Basic Transactions

### Deposits

![Mobile Banking](../images/phone/using%20phone2.jpg)

*Every deposit is a step forward*

**At a Bank:**
1. Walk up to the counter
2. Interact with the teller
3. Select "Deposit"
4. Enter amount
5. Confirm

**At an ATM:**
1. Approach the ATM
2. Insert card (automatic)
3. Select "Deposit"
4. Enter amount
5. Take receipt

| Deposit Type | Processing | Limit |
|--------------|------------|-------|
| **Cash** | Instant | None |
| **Check** | May have hold | Varies |
| **Transfer In** | Instant | None |

### Withdrawals

Need cash? Here's how:

**Bank Counter:**
- Larger amounts available
- No transaction fees
- Full service

**ATM:**
- Quick access
- Available 24/7
- Some may have limits

> **Pro Tip:** Don't withdraw more than you need. The walk from the ATM to your car is when you're most vulnerable.

## Transfers & Payments

### Sending Money

Transfer funds to other players:

1. Access banking (bank/ATM/phone)
2. Select "Transfer"
3. Enter recipient's ID or name
4. Enter amount
5. Confirm transaction

| Transfer Type | Speed | Fee |
|---------------|-------|-----|
| **To Player** | Instant | None |
| **To Business** | Instant | None |
| **Bill Payment** | Instant | None |

> **RP Scenario:**
> *"The money's in your account." You refresh the banking app. There it is - $50,000, just like he promised. "Pleasure doing business," you say into the phone. "The shipment arrives tonight." You hang up. Clean transaction. No paper trail. The way business should be.*

### Receiving Money

When someone sends you money:
- Notification on your phone
- Funds instantly available
- Check transaction history for details

## Security & Safety

### Protecting Your Wealth

| Do | Don't |
|----|-------|
| Bank immediately after earning | Carry large cash amounts |
| Use ATMs in safe areas | Withdraw at night in sketchy spots |
| Check surroundings before transactions | Count money in public |
| Log out of banking apps | Share account information |

### ATM Safety

Some ATMs are safer than others:

| Location | Safety Level |
|----------|--------------|
| **Inside banks** | Safest |
| **Police stations** | Very safe |
| **Gas stations** | Medium |
| **Remote areas** | Risky |
| **Dark alleys** | Asking for trouble |

> **The Setup:**
> *The ATM beeps. Transaction complete. You turn to leave and there they are - three guys, spread out to block the exits. "That was a nice deposit," one says. "Why don't you take it back out?" Your heart sinks. Wrong ATM. Wrong time.*

## Common Mistakes

### What NOT to Do

| Mistake | Consequence |
|---------|-------------|
| Carrying all your cash | One robbery = broke |
| Public withdrawals | Target on your back |
| Ignoring notifications | Miss important payments |
| Lending without records | "What money?" |
| Trusting strangers | Scams are real |

> **The Lesson:**
> *"I'll pay you back tomorrow." That was three weeks ago. You've seen him driving a new car, flashing cash at the club. But your $10,000? Gone. No receipt. No record. Just a promise from someone you thought was a friend.*

## Tips for Success

### Starting Out
1. **Deposit everything** - Build the habit
2. **Check balance regularly** - Know where you stand
3. **Set savings goals** - Work toward something

### Growing Wealth
1. **Multiple income streams** - Don't rely on one job
2. **Invest in assets** - Property, business
3. **Track expenses** - Know where money goes

### Protecting Wealth
1. **Minimal cash on hand** - Only what you need
2. **Safe banking locations** - Choose wisely
3. **Trusted transfers only** - Verify before sending
