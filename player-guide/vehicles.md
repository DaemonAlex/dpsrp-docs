# Vehicles Guide

Everything you need to know about vehicles in DPSRP.

## Getting Your First Vehicle

### Dealerships
- **Premium Deluxe Motorsport (PDM)** - Standard vehicles
- **Luxury Autos** - High-end cars
- **Boat/Air Dealerships** - Watercraft and aircraft

### Process
1. Visit a dealership
2. Browse available inventory
3. Test drive (if available)
4. Purchase with cash or financing
5. Vehicle added to your garage

## Garages

### Storing Vehicles
Your vehicles are stored in personal garages. Access them at:
- House garages
- Public parking structures
- Job-specific garages

### Garage Menu (jg-advancedgarages)
- View all owned vehicles
- Store/retrieve vehicles
- Check vehicle status
- Transfer vehicles

## Fuel System (qs-fuel)

### Refueling
1. Pull up to a gas pump
2. Exit vehicle
3. Interact with pump
4. Select fuel amount
5. Pay and pump

### Fuel Tips
- Different vehicles have different tank sizes
- Running out of fuel strands you
- Jerry cans available for emergencies
- Electric vehicles use charging stations

## Vehicle Features

### Basic Controls
| Key | Action |
|-----|--------|
| F | Enter/Exit |
| G | Seatbelt |
| H | Headlights |
| Q/E | Turn signals |
| X | Roll down window |
| L | Lock/Unlock |

### Vehicle Control Menu (CodeSign)
Press **K** or type `/vehcontrol` to access the full vehicle control UI.

**Available Inside Vehicle:**
| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Engine Toggle | Turn engine on/off |
| Indicators | Left/right turn signals |
| Doors | Open/close all doors |
| Windows | Control all windows |
| Extras | Toggle vehicle add-ons |
| Liveries | Change paint schemes |
| Neons | Underglow lighting & colors |
| Seats | Switch seat positions |

**Available Outside Vehicle:**
- Limited options when standing near your vehicle
- Must be the vehicle owner

### Vehicle Add-ons (Extras)
Many vehicles have toggleable extras:
- Lightbars
- Roof racks
- Mirrors
- Antenna
- Accessories

### Neon Underglow
Customize your underglow through the vehicle menu:
- Multiple color options
- Toggle on/off
- Some colors may be restricted

## Vehicle Keys & Locking (wasabi_carlock)

Advanced vehicle key system with animations and security.

### Basic Controls
| Key | Action |
|-----|--------|
| L | Lock/Unlock vehicle |
| G | Toggle engine |

### Key Ownership
- You automatically receive keys to all owned vehicles on spawn
- Keys are synced server-side (anti-cheat protected)
- Full animation support for all interactions

### Giving Keys to Others
Share vehicle access with other players:
1. Use the give keys command
2. Select player from menu
3. They can now unlock/start your vehicle

### Vehicle Theft (Criminal)

**Lockpicking**
- Use lockpicks on locked doors
- Skill check required
- Difficulty based on vehicle class:
  | Class | Difficulty |
  |-------|------------|
  | Sedans | Easier |
  | Sports | Medium |
  | Supercars | Harder |

**Hotwiring**
- Start engine without keys
- Skill check required
- Limited attempts before failure
- Difficulty scales with vehicle value

**Robbing NPCs**
- Hold NPC at gunpoint
- Demand their car keys
- Risk vs reward gameplay

### Protecting Your Vehicle
1. **Always lock** when leaving
2. **Park smart** - safe areas, garages
3. **Use garages** - stored vehicles are safe
4. **Don't leave running** - easy target

## Mileage System (jg-vehiclemileage)

Your vehicle tracks:
- Total miles driven
- Affects resale value
- May affect maintenance needs

## Insurance & Impound

### Insurance
- Protects against total loss
- Claim at insurance office
- Deductible may apply

### Impound
If your vehicle is impounded:
1. Visit the impound lot
2. Pay impound fee
3. Retrieve your vehicle

Vehicles left too long may be auctioned.

## Tips

1. **Start cheap** - Build up to better vehicles
2. **Lock your car** - Prevent theft
3. **Maintain regularly** - Cheaper than repairs
4. **Insure valuable vehicles** - Protect your investment
5. **Watch your speed** - Police enforce traffic laws
6. **Keep fuel topped off** - Don't get stranded
