# Phone Guide

Your lb-phone is essential for life in Los Santos.

## Accessing Your Phone

- Press **F1** to open phone
- Swipe and tap to navigate
- Press **F1** again or **Escape** to close

## Core Apps

### Calls & Messages
- **Phone** - Make and receive calls
- **Messages** - Text other players
- **Contacts** - Save phone numbers

### Finance
- **Bank** - Check balance, transfer money
- **Crypto** - Cryptocurrency trading

### Social
- **Twitter/Birdy** - Social media posts
- **Instagram/Instapic** - Photo sharing
- **Yellow Pages** - Business directory

### Utilities
- **Maps/GPS** - Navigation
- **Camera** - Take photos
- **Gallery** - View saved photos
- **Settings** - Phone customization

### Services
- **Taxi** - Request a ride
- **Mechanic** - Call for repairs
- **Emergency** - 911 services
- **Tow** - Request tow truck

## Making Calls

1. Open Phone app
2. Dial number or select contact
3. Wait for answer
4. Talk (use voice chat)
5. Hang up when done

## Sending Messages

1. Open Messages app
2. Start new conversation or select existing
3. Type your message
4. Send

## Emergency Services

### 911
- Police, EMS, Fire
- Describe your emergency
- Give your location
- Wait for response

### Specific Departments
- **Police** - Crime reports, emergencies
- **EMS** - Medical emergencies
- **Fire** - Fire emergencies

## Job-Specific Features

Some jobs have special phone integrations:
- **Taxi** - Receive fare requests
- **Mechanic** - Service calls
- **Delivery** - Order notifications

## Tips

1. **Save important contacts** - Quick access when needed
2. **Check messages regularly** - Don't miss important info
3. **Use GPS** - Navigate to destinations easily
4. **911 for emergencies** - Real emergencies only
