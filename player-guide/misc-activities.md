## Roller Skating

### Getting Started
1. Find a skate rental location
2. Rent or purchase skates
3. Equip your skates
4. Hit the boardwalk!

### Skate Controls
| Key | Action |
|-----|--------|
| W | Skate forward |
| S | Slow down |
| A/D | Turn |
| Space | Jump |
| Shift | Sprint |

### Skating Spots
- Vespucci Beach boardwalk
- Del Perro Pier
- Skate parks
- Any smooth surface!

### Tips
- Great for roleplay meetups
- Explore the beach areas
- Challenge friends to races
- Good exercise RP

## Hitchhiking (BigDaddy-Hitchhike)

### How It Works
Need a ride but can't afford a taxi?

### Getting a Ride
1. Stand near a road
2. Use hitchhike emote/action
3. Wait for NPC or player to stop
4. Get in when offered
5. Set your destination

### Tips
- Works best on main roads
- Be patient - not instant
- Thank your driver (RP!)
- Safer than walking long distances

---

## Ride Service (BigDaddy-RideService)

### What Is It?
Player-driven transportation service - like being an Uber driver!

### As a Driver
1. Sign up for ride service job
2. Go on duty
3. Accept ride requests
4. Pick up passengers
5. Drive to destination
6. Get paid

### As a Passenger
1. Request ride via phone
2. Wait for driver acceptance
3. Get picked up
4. Ride to destination
5. Pay fare

### Pricing
- Based on distance
- Surge pricing during busy times
- Tips appreciated!
