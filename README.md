## Key Features

### Vehicle Systems
- **jg-dealerships** - Vehicle purchasing with 10% down, 12-payment financing
- **jg-mechanic** - Full tuning, nitrous, engine swaps
- **jg-advancedgarages** - Advanced garage management
- **CodeSign Vehicle Control** - Press K for full vehicle menu
- **wasabi_carlock** - Vehicle locking, lockpicking, hotwiring

### Business Systems
- **lc_factories** - Coca-Cola, vehicles, electronics, weapons production
- **lc_truck_logistics** - 36-level trucking career with leaderboards
- **qb-management** - Boss menus for whitelisted jobs

### Legal Systems
- **ap-court** - Full court system with bar exam
- **ap-government** - City government and taxation
- **wasabi_mdt** - Police mobile data terminal

### Core Systems
- **wasabi_multichar** - 4 character slots, VIP extras
- **wasabi_spawn** - 10 spawn locations across San Andreas
- **wasabi_crutch** - Mobility aids (crutches, wheelchairs)

### Entertainment
- **rcore_dragrace** - Quarter-mile drag racing
- **Pata_RcRace** - RC car racing
- Multiple RCore activities (arcade, bowling, golf, etc.)
